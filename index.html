Hi guys , my name is Leo Monu .
I am here to show all the games I have created. Click on the games you want to play. These games are best played in laptop or PC.

Alien Runner Game

This is an Alien Game.It is similar to the T-rex runner game. Press space to make the alien jump.Avoid the cactus and Landmines. I Dare you to score 200 points.

Space Shooter Game

This is a Space Game.Press space to relese bullets. Shoot the enemy spaceship to get points. Dont let your score reach 0. When you reach 0,then click the red button to play the game again.

Maze Runner Game

This is a Maze Game.Get the Parrot to the other side of the area. Use the arrow keys to move the Parrot. Do not let the Parrot touch the ice mountains.

Soccer Game

This is a Soccer Game.Use your mouse to move up and down. Do not let the ball cross you.Press K for kick animation(it's optional). Race to 5 points!!

Falling Object Game

This is a Falling Object Game.Using your mouse, click on the elephant and avoid the monster. After an object crosses half of the way, you don't get the point.
